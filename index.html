About Us

Bangladisham Saotal is a digital platform committed to preserving and promoting the heritage, history, and cultural practices of the indigenous Santal people in Bangladesh. Through educational videos, blogs, and multilingual storytelling (Bangla & English), we aim to keep this rich culture alive for future generations.
